What is a Manufactured Home?

Quality Built. Affordably Priced.

A manufactured home is a factory-built residence constructed under the federal HUD code — the national building standard for manufactured housing. These homes are built entirely in a controlled factory environment, then transported to your chosen home site.

At Mobile View Properties, we carry a wide selection of manufactured homes ranging from single-section starter homes to spacious multi-section designs with premium finishes. Our expert team assists you from selection through delivery and installation.

Why Choose Manufactured?

Smart Value. Real Quality.

Manufactured homes deliver exceptional value for buyers who want a quality home without the high cost of traditional construction. Here is why thousands of families choose manufactured housing every year.

Significant Cost Savings
Manufactured homes typically cost 30–50% less per square foot than site-built homes, making home ownership genuinely accessible for first-time buyers and growing families.
Fast Move-In Timeline
Factory construction can be completed in weeks rather than months. Once your site is prepared, your home can be delivered and installed quickly — so you move in sooner.
Consistent Build Quality
Factory assembly eliminates weather delays and quality inconsistencies common to on-site builds. Every home is inspected at multiple stages before leaving the factory.
Customisation Options
Choose your floor plan, exterior style, kitchen layout, flooring, cabinets, and more. Modern manufactured homes offer an impressive range of personalisation options.
Modern Design

Built for Real Life

Today's manufactured homes bear little resemblance to their predecessors. Open floor plans, vaulted ceilings, premium kitchen and bathroom finishes, and energy-efficient construction are now standard — not upgrades.

Our homes are designed to suit every lifestyle and land type: permanent foundations, rural acreage, or land-lease communities. Mobile View Properties handles site evaluation, logistics, and delivery coordination globally.
