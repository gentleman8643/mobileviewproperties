Park Models & Cabins

Your Home, Wherever Life Takes You

Park model homes and cabins are compact, full-featured residences designed for resort communities, campgrounds, and private land. Certified as recreational vehicles, they deliver the warm feel of a permanent home with the freedom and flexibility of a smaller footprint.

At Mobile View Properties, we deliver park model homes to communities across North America and beyond. Each unit is built to exacting quality standards, fully furnished, and ready to place at your destination of choice.

Features

Everything You Need, Nothing You Don't

Park models and cabins are engineered to maximise liveable space. Thoughtful layouts and premium finishes make every square foot count.

Full Kitchen
Fully equipped kitchens with modern appliances, ample storage, and quality countertops make preparing meals a pleasure wherever you are.
Private Bathroom
Full or 3/4 bathrooms with walk-in showers, vanity storage, and quality fixtures provide the comfort and privacy of a permanent home.
Outdoor Living
Optional deck additions, screened porches, and exterior finishes that complement any natural surroundings — from forest retreats to lakeside communities.
